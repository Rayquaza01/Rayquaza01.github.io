February 21, 2024 | Tags: personal programming software

History Cleaner is 7 years old!

The first version was created on February 8, 2017. I originally created it to be a WebExtension port of Expire history by days. This was when Firefox was first switching to WebExtensions and removing support for XUL extensions.
